     CHILDHOOD YEARS

     I live in Real in the Province of Quezon since birth and I can tell that this is the best place that I can recommend for the tourists.1 From a very young age I really wanted go outside to play with my neighbors.2 My mother told me not to go or play outside but to take a nap instead.3 I have three siblings and I am the only son.4 I felt alone because I don't want to play dolls or girl toys with them.5 One time, I tried to sneak when my mother's friend visited her.6 I thought that was the best chance to play outside, but my older sister guard the front door so I had no choice but to go back to sleep.7 I can't also escape through the back door because that's a forest and dead end. 8 So, I am so happy when they allow me to play with my Friends in the neighborhood.9 My family business was buying coconuts to make it copra, and they always make me along with our jeepney.10 Sometimes we fought because of nonsense that make us cry. 11 The business went well with the help of our laborer which is pahinante in tagalog.12 Our family business had a strong and good relationship with our customers.13 We went in different and beautiful places with my family from north to south and I can say that I am proud to have this kind of family.14 On every vacation, our parents sent me and my sisters to my grandmother's place to help her and to bond with our cousins, because most of our cousins live there.15 I played there with many children with good memories that helps me to improve my socialization with other people.16 I remember that my mother told that my lola was very proud of me with the achievements I received since I started schooling.17 Years gone by, my grandmother died of a heart attack.18 All of us was shocked because of the incident, but the great memories remind us that she always by our side no matter what happen.19 I can't say that my childhood was great, but I need to accept that someday they need to leave unexpectedly in this world.20
